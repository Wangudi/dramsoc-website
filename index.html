About DRAMSOC

Passionate performers, dedicated to excellence

Our Mission

The Strathmore Drama Society (DRAMSOC) is dedicated to fostering creativity, building confidence, and creating unforgettable theatrical experiences. We provide a platform for students to explore their artistic talents and develop their performance skills.

What We Offer

  • Professional theater training and workshops
  • Original and classic play productions
  • Acting, directing, and technical theater skills development
  • Performance opportunities for all skill levels
  • Collaborative creative environment
  • Community outreach programs
