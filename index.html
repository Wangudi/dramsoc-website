About Us

Welcome to DRAMSOC – The Strathmore Drama Society, where creativity meets community. We are a passionate group of actors, directors, stage managers, and storytellers dedicated to bringing the magic of theatre to life at Strathmore University.

From powerful stage performances to behind-the-scenes creativity, DRAMSOC is more than a club—it's a family. Whether you're an experienced performer or curious about theatre for the first time, we welcome all who share a love for storytelling and artistic expression.

Our Mission: To foster creativity, collaboration, and community through the transformative power of drama and the arts.

Our Vision: To be a leading creative hub at Strathmore University, inspiring students to explore their talents and share meaningful stories.
