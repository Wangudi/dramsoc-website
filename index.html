About DRAMSOC

The Strathmore Drama Society (DRAMSOC) is a vibrant community of passionate performers, directors, writers, and theater enthusiasts. We are dedicated to creating memorable theatrical experiences and fostering artistic growth among our members.

Our Mission

To inspire, educate, and entertain through the art of theater while providing a platform for creative expression.

We produce plays, workshops, and events that showcase the diverse talents of Strathmore University students.
